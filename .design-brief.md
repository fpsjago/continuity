## Core
- **Name:** CONTINUITY  *(real networking term — the continuity test proving a cable carries signal end-to-end; brand promise = uninterrupted signal)*
- **Type:** Marketing template for sale — top tier ($149+), multipage
- **Purpose:** Site for an infrastructure/security-hardware house. Visitors = IT directors, network/SRE engineers, integrators, procurement — arrive **skeptical of marketing fluff**, trust **measured precision**. Leave thinking *"these people measure before they speak — certified, not claimed."*
- **Aesthetic Tone:** **Engineering monograph on warm paper.** The DEFAULT register is a **warm-paper blueprint / spec-sheet** — the document an engineer actually trusts: bone paper, graphite ink, drafting marks, title blocks, caliper rules, printed-datasheet rigor. A single **dramatic dark "Equipment Room" counter-section** (the 2am rack-room) provides contrast — the dark↔paper duality is a *designed moment*, not the dominant mode. (This INVERTS the original dark-default to break the studio's dark-build streak and is more on-concept: datasheets are printed on paper.)
- **The Unforgettable (THE one signature):** **THE LIVE SIGNAL PULSE.** A single 2px ember filament drawn as a blueprint cable route; a glowing pulse-head rides it, **scroll-DRIVEN (reads scroll position; does NOT own the scrollbar — native scroll/anchors/find-in-page all keep working).** Each section is a node it energizes: hero patch-panel port → a 24-port switch lights left-to-right → a cable **explodes into its cross-section** (jacket→foil→pairs→OFC copper core) teaching real spec → a CCTV reticle tracks it → a door-controller seats → at the CTA the connector **seats with one hardware-snap** and the readout resolves `LINK ESTABLISHED · 99.997% · 9.4 Gbps`. Ember + copper signal on warm paper = novel (not the cliché ember-on-black).
  - **Architecture (LOCKED, council):** NOT one monolithic full-page SVG path. **Per-section node-anchored path SEGMENTS**, each with its own IntersectionObserver-gated GSAP timeline + local debounced ResizeObserver; geometry routed through **measured DOM rects** (not absolute coords). Recompute on `document.fonts.ready` + image `decode()` + every CMS-list/Flip render (`ScrollTrigger.refresh()` same frame). Driven off the **Lenis ticker**. Ignore iOS URL-bar (width-only resize compare). The illusion of continuity, segment-handoff at section edges — never literal one-path fragility.

## Secondary (subordinate — NOT a competing signature)
- **Index readout**: a hairline left-margin plate index (`PLATE 01 CABLES…`) + registration-crosshair that snaps to section baselines — driven off the **SAME single ScrollTrigger** as the pulse, doubles as nav + progress + mobile wayfinding. **Drop the variable-font weight-scrub** (not GPU-composited; tanks Lighthouse) — use color/opacity weight illusion instead. Must be real focusable keyboard/aria nav links.

## Typography — exactly 2 families (council fix)
- **Heading Font:** Bricolage Grotesque
- **Body Font:** IBM Plex Sans (with IBM Plex Mono as the same-superfamily data/spec utility)
- **Heading:** **Bricolage Grotesque** (600/700) — warm, engineered, slightly characterful grotesque; oversized 120–200px openers, mixed weights, -0.02em.
- **Body + Data:** **IBM Plex** superfamily — **Plex Sans** for prose, **Plex Mono** for the spec/data/readout layer (gauges, frequencies, plate numbers, status tokens). One designed family system covering both roles = compliant with exactly-2. *(If a serif body is wanted later, swap Plex Sans→Source Serif 4 and keep Plex Mono as the lone data face — but default ships clean 2-family.)*
- Anti-convergence: not used in this session's prior work. ✓

## Hero
- **Pattern:** H-kinetic-split (left-locked oversized word + scroll energize) — NEVER centered text+button.
- **Hero Scene:** *Open on near-empty warm paper, a faint 12-col blueprint grid + corner registration crosshairs, mono folio `CONTINUITY / INFRASTRUCTURE INDEX / EDITION 2026` setting letter-by-letter. Center-left, `CONTINUITY` hangs in ~200px Bricolage with a flat oscilloscope line struck through it. A jewelry-lit macro connector (real Pexels) sits mid-ground. On load the flat-line breaks into a live ember waveform, the pulse is born at a hand-drawn patch-panel port and seats into the connector; a `STANDBY → LINK UP` mono token flips. On scroll the pulse begins its journey; a caliper rule draws itself under the hero.*
- **Hero Scroll Effect:** scroll-driven `--progress`; pulse position reads scrollY; connector parallax + macro zoom 1.0→1.06; segment handoff to section 2.
